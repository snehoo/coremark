What is Science at Cambridge Lower Secondary?

Science boosters are single-topic Cambridge Lower Secondary practice packs covering Biology, Chemistry and Physics — each with a cheat sheet, 20 leveled questions and full worked solutions.

  • 🧬 Biology — cells, body systems, reproduction, ecosystems and life processes
  • ⚗️ Chemistry — particles, elements, reactions and the periodic table
  • ⚡ Physics — forces, energy, electricity, light and sound

How do I use a Science booster?

  1. Read the cheat sheet first. All the key facts and formulas for the topic, in 5 minutes.
  2. Attempt all 20 questions. Work through Foundation → Standard → Stretch in order — each band builds on the last.
  3. Check with the worked solutions. Read the error flags — they show the mistakes Cambridge examiners see most often.
  4. Use the parent and tutor guide. Score bands and study tips tell you what to focus on next.

A science booster is a focused practice pack covering one topic from the Cambridge Lower Secondary Science syllabus (Stages 7, 8 or 9). Each pack contains a one-page cheat sheet, 20 levelled questions across Biology, Chemistry or Physics, full worked solutions, and a parent guide written for non-science-expert parents.
Cambridge Lower Secondary Science Checkpoint rewards precise use of scientific vocabulary and structured written answers. A science booster isolates one topic so students can practise the specific content and command words examiners look for, rather than revising broadly across all three sciences at once.
